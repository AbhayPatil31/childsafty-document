Election Pulse (developed by Rudra Research) is fully committed to maintaining a safe, child-friendly environment and preventing **child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)** and **child sexual abuse material (CSAM)**.

### Prohibited Content & Behavior
We strictly prohibit any content, actions, or materials that:
- Depict, promote, or facilitate the **sexual abuse or exploitation of children**
- Contain, distribute, or link to **child sexual abuse material (CSAM)**
- Involve **grooming**, solicitation, or any form of endangerment of minors
- Encourage or depict harmful behavior toward children

### Our Enforcement Actions
- All user-generated content (polls, surveys, comments, uploads) is monitored.
- Any suspected CSAE/CSAM will be **removed immediately**.
- Confirmed violations are reported to relevant authorities, including the **National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)** and/or local law enforcement (e.g., in India).

### User Reporting
Users are strongly encouraged to report any concerning content via:
- In-app "Report" button/feature (available on polls/comments)
- Email: [rudraresearchanalytics@gmail.com]

### Compliance
Election Pulse complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations (including relevant sections of Indian IT Act and international standards). We do not target children as primary users, but we take all necessary steps to protect minors who may access the app.

This policy is reviewed regularly and may be updated. For questions, contact: rudraresearchanalytics@gmail.com

Definitions of CSAE and CSAM follow standards from Google Play and legal authorities (see: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/14747720).
